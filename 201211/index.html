Friday, December 11, 2020

Arashiyama Lightup・嵐山花灯路

Workers have been stringing lights all over Arashiyama for the past several weeks, and tonight they turned them on for the Arashiyama Hanatouro, which lasts until Dec 20.

数週間の準備の後、12月20日まで続く京都嵐山花灯路のために嵐山中のライトが点灯しました。

Taken in Arashiyama, Kyoto, Japan
