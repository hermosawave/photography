About

Thank you for visiting my website.

I have been working in digital media for the past 40 years: music synthesizers & drum machines; sound recording; laser light shows; print, video, multimedia and web design; and photography. These days I am a photographer. I shoot primarily landscapes and architecture. My principal clients are architects and real estate agents. My photographs have been published in magazines and newspapers, in the US, Japan and elsewhere. Fine art prints are available from this website as well as galleries.

I hope you enjoy these images.
