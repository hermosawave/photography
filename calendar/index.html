Hermosawave 2024 Calendar: Images of Japan

A selection of photographs by Daniel Sofer throughout Japan, from Tohoku to Kyushu. Scenery of urban and rural Japan; flowers and autumn leaves.

ダニエル・ソーファーが東北から九州まで日本各地で撮影した写真の数々。 日本の都会と田舎の風景、花と紅葉。

My 17th annual calendar is a collection of images taken throughout Japan, from Akita to Miyazaki. A small edition, printed in Kyoto. Calendars are available now for immediate shipping. I hope you enjoy it.
